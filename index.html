Looking for a football graphic designer in Manchester?

Whether you’re a grassroots football club, youth academy, Sunday League team or community football organisation, professional design can help your club stand out, attract players, impress sponsors and engage supporters.

I’m John Wright, a Manchester-based graphic designer specialising in football club graphics, branding and promotional artwork for grassroots football clubs throughout Manchester, Greater Manchester and across the UK.
Football is built on passion, pride and community. Every club has its own story, and every badge, colour and matchday graphic should reflect that identity. My goal is to create eye-catching, professional football graphics that capture the spirit of your club while helping you build a strong and recognisable brand both online and offline.
