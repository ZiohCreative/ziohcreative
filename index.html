ABOUT ME

John Wright is talented Designer based in Manchester, specializing in Football Club Graphics. Creating vibrant and engaging graphics for grassroots football clubs. Understanding the unique spirit and community values of these clubs, John approaches each project with a passion for both design and the beautiful game. His work captures the essence of local teams, whether through eye-catching logos, promotional materials, or merchandise designs that resonate with players and fans alike. From the initial concept to the final artwork, John’s creative process is thorough and collaborative. He takes the time to understand each club's identity, mission, and aspirations, ensuring that his designs reflect their unique stories and community ties. By blending innovative design techniques with an appreciation for the tradition of grassroots football, John Wright produces football club graphics that not only stand out but also foster a sense of belonging among players and supporters. His dedication to quality and creativity makes him a sought-after graphic designer for all grassroots and football clubs looking to elevate their brand and engage their audience effectively.

John Wright Football Club Graphics


In summary, the array of artwork, from logo and poster designs to awards and promotional materials, not only serves practical purposes but also weaves the identity of local football clubs into the fabric of their communities, allowing them to flourish and create lasting memories.
